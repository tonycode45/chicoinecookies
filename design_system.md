# Design System – Chicoine Cookies

## Brand Aesthetic
Warm, premium bakery vibes:
- Creams
- Browns
- Pastel accents
- Soft shadows
- Big appetizing cookie imagery

## Color Palette
- **Cookie Brown:** #C69C6D  
- **Cream:** #F7E9D7  
- **Pastel Yellow:** #FFE3B3  
- **Dark Cocoa:** #3A2E28  
- **Soft White:** #FFF9F3  

## Typography
### Headlines:
- Fraunces or Playfair Display  
- Weight: 600–700  
- Elegant serif with personality

### Body:
- Inter or Work Sans  
- Clean, modern, readable

## Buttons
### Primary:
- Brown background (#C69C6D)
- White text
- Rounded-xl

### Secondary:
- Cream background (#F7E9D7)
- Brown text
- Rounded-xl

### Ghost:
- Transparent w/ brown border

## Components to Standardize
- Product card (image → name → price → add to cart)
- Subscription plan card
- Review card
- Countdown timer
- Drag & drop box builder tiles
- Timeline story blocks
- Loyalty progress bar
- Quiz step cards

## Imagery
- Warm lighting
- Close-ups of cookies
- Real textures (crumbs, chocolate, etc.)
- Soft backgrounds

## Spacing Rules
- Big breathing room  
- 24–48px padding blocks  
- Large hero sections
