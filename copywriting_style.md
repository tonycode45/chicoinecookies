# Copywriting Style Guide – Chicoine Cookies

## Brand Voice
Warm, premium, delicious, friendly, and slightly playful.

## Tone
- Honest and inviting
- Sensory and flavorful
- Confident but not arrogant
- Modern direct-to-consumer style

## Vocabulary Themes
- Warmth: melt, fresh, cozy, baked, warm, soft, oven
- Sensory: gooey, crunchy, velvety, buttery, rich
- Joy: treat, delight, indulge, enjoy, share

## Writing Rules
1. Always speak directly to the customer ("you").
2. Keep sentences short and delicious.
3. Always focus on benefits, not features.
4. Use sensory language to describe flavors.
5. Use strong CTAs like:
   - “Order your box”
   - “Pick your flavors”
   - “Start your subscription”

## Example Header Lines
- “Freshly baked happiness in every bite.”
- “Build the cookie box of your dreams.”
- “Treat yourself. You deserve it.”

## Example Product Description Style
“Soft, warm, and loaded with melty chocolate chunks. Our OG Classic Cookie melts in your mouth and hits every sweet craving perfectly.”
