# Project Overview – Chicoine Cookies

Chicoine Cookies is a premium artisanal cookie brand with a fully featured online platform.

## Mission
Deliver the best-tasting cookies with the best possible digital experience — premium, warm, fun, and modern.

## Core Objectives
- Convert visitors into buyers with an elite UX/UI experience.
- Make ordering cookies feel fun, smooth, and appetizing.
- Build long-term customer loyalty through subscriptions and rewards.
- Provide an unforgettable visual brand identity.

## Website Pillars
1. E-commerce (browse → add to cart → checkout)
2. Subscription model (“monthly cookie box”)
3. Brand storytelling (founder story, behind-the-scenes)
4. Engagement features (quiz, reviews, UGC, loyalty)
5. Premium aesthetic and trust-building visuals

## Tone & Voice
- Warm, friendly, slightly playful  
- Premium and professional  
- Food-centric, sensory language  
- Clear CTAs and UX writing
